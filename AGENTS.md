# Legal Repository Guidance

- This repository is the sole canonical source for General Translation's public legal documents.
- Keep published documents as plain Markdown under locale directories such as `en-US/`.
- Follow the locale's `_template.md` frontmatter schema and use exactly one H1 title per document.
- Use ISO `YYYY-MM-DD` dates for `last_updated`.
- Do not add private agreements, signatures, privileged counsel material, customer-specific terms, or internal-only notes.
- Do not edit copies inside consumer repositories. Update their pinned submodule commits after merging here.
- Legal meaning must not be changed without qualified review.
